<p><p>The motorized ball valves market encompasses various types, including two-way and three-way ball valves, along with other variants. Two-way ball valves control fluid flow from one inlet to one outlet, providing simple on/off functionality. Three-way ball valves can direct flow between two outlets, allowing for mixing or diverting fluids. Other types may include specialized valves serving specific applications. The choice between these types depends on system requirements, including flow management, space constraints, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
