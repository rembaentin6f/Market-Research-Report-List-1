<p><p>The pre-filled syringe market is segmented into two primary types: glass pre-filled syringes and plastic pre-filled syringes. Glass pre-filled syringes offer high compatibility with various drugs, excellent barrier properties, and are often preferred for high-value biologics. Conversely, plastic pre-filled syringes are lighter, shatterproof, and often more cost-effective, making them suitable for vaccines and smaller-volume applications. Each type serves specific needs in the pharmaceutical industry, influencing production, storage, and user experience in drug delivery systems.</p></p>
