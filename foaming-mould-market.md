<p><ul><li>EPS Foaming Mould</li><li>EPP Foaming Mould</li><li>PU Foaming Mould</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foaming mould market encompasses several key types, primarily including EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) foaming moulds, EPP (Expanded Polypropylene) foaming moulds, and PU (Polyurethane) foaming moulds. EPS foaming moulds are lightweight and recyclable, making them popular in packaging and construction. EPP foaming moulds offer enhanced durability and impact resistance, suitable for automotive components and protective packaging. PU foaming moulds are versatile, providing excellent insulation and cushioning properties, widely used in furniture, packaging, and construction applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
