<p><p>Visible Light Communication (VLC) is a communication technology that uses visible light to transmit data. It leverages LEDs (Light Emitting Diodes) to send information by modulating the light intensity at high speeds, allowing for data transmission without the need for traditional radio frequency signals. VLC is increasingly gaining attention for its potential applications in areas such as indoor lighting, automotive communications, and smart cities, thanks to its ability to provide high-speed, secure data transmission.</p><p>The Visible Light Communication Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by advancements in LED technology and an increasing demand for high-speed communication systems across various sectors. Key trends include the integration of VLC with existing wireless technologies, enhanced security features due to light's inherent directionality, and the rise of intelligent transportation systems that utilize VLC for vehicle-to-vehicle and vehicle-to-infrastructure communication. Additionally, as smart lighting solutions become more prevalent, the incorporation of VLC in urban infrastructure offers significant growth opportunities in the market, positioning VLC as a complementary technology to traditional wireless communications.</p></p>
