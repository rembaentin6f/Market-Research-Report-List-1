<p><p>The Oral X-Ray Machines market encompasses two main types: Analog X-Ray and Digital X-Ray systems. Analog X-Rays utilize traditional film-based technology to capture images of teeth and oral structures, offering a straightforward, cost-effective solution but requiring longer processing times. Digital X-Rays, on the other hand, employ advanced sensors to generate high-resolution images instantly, enhancing diagnostic capabilities and reducing radiation exposure. The shift towards digital systems is driven by their efficiency, image quality, and integration with modern dental practices.</p></p>
