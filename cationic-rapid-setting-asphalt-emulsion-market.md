<p><ul><li>CRS-1</li><li>CRS-2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cationic Rapid Setting Asphalt Emulsion (CRS) is primarily classified into two types: CRS-1 and CRS-2. CRS-1 is designed for quick curing and is typically used for surface treatments like chip seals, providing a fast set time ideal for light traffic. CRS-2, on the other hand, has a slightly longer setting time and is suitable for heavier applications, such as base stabilization and thicker overlays. Both types facilitate effective adhesion and durability in paving projects.</p></p>
