<p><p>The Low Density PTFE Tapes market is segmented into ordinary and high temperature resistance types. Ordinary PTFE tapes are commonly used for sealing threads and joints in plumbing and HVAC applications, offering basic resistance to chemicals and moisture. In contrast, high temperature resistance PTFE tapes are designed to endure elevated temperatures, making them suitable for industrial processes and environments where heat exposure is significant, such as in aerospace and automotive applications, ensuring durability and reliability under extreme conditions.</p></p>
