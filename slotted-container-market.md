<p><p>The slotted container market consists of three main types: Half Slotted Containers (HSC), Regular Slotted Containers (RSC), and Overlap Slotted Containers (OSC). HSCs feature open tops and can be easily stacked, making them ideal for display packaging. RSCs have flaps that meet in the center, providing sturdy protection during shipping. OSCs have overlapping flaps for added strength and structural integrity. These containers cater to various industries, optimizing storage, transportation, and presentation of products effectively.</p></p>
