<p><p>Male hypogonadism is primarily treated through two market types: Testosterone Replacement Therapy (TRT) and Gonadotropin-Releasing Hormone (GnRH) Therapy. TRT involves supplementing testosterone to restore normal levels, providing relief from symptoms like fatigue and low libido. It can be administered via injections, gels, or patches. On the other hand, GnRH Therapy stimulates the pituitary gland to produce hormones that boost testosterone production naturally. Each therapy caters to different underlying causes and patient needs in managing male hypogonadism.</p></p>
