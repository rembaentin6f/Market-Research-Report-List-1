<p><p>Whole Transcriptomics Sequencing encompasses various market types, primarily including PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction), microarray, and sequencing techniques. PCR amplifies specific RNA segments for analysis, making it ideal for targeted studies. Microarrays allow simultaneous examination of thousands of transcripts, enabling broad gene expression profiling. In contrast, sequencing provides comprehensive insights into the entire transcriptome, offering an in-depth view of gene expression, splicing variations, and novel transcripts. Each method serves distinct research needs, driving advancements in genomics and personalized medicine.</p></p>
