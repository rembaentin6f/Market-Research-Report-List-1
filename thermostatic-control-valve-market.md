<p><strong>The Thermostatic Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermostatic Mixing Valves</li><li>Thermostatic Diverting Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermostatic control valve market features two main types: thermostatic mixing valves and thermostatic diverting valves. Thermostatic mixing valves blend hot and cold water to maintain a consistent temperature, ensuring safety and comfort in plumbing systems. In contrast, thermostatic diverting valves direct fluid flow between different circuits, optimizing heating or cooling in various applications. Both types enhance system efficiency, promote energy savings, and improve performance by automatically adjusting flow based on temperature changes, catering to residential and industrial needs.</p></p>
