<p><p>The Electronic Anti-Reflection Glass market is categorized into two primary types: Chemical Treatment and Physical Processing. Chemical Treatment involves applying specialized coatings to glass surfaces, enhancing light transmission and minimizing reflections through chemical reactions. In contrast, Physical Processing encompasses techniques like surface texturing or laser engraving, which physically alter the glass's surface to disrupt light wave patterns, thereby reducing glare. Both methods aim to improve visibility and performance in electronic displays and optical devices, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
