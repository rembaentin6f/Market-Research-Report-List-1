<p><ul><li>Fly Ash Base</li><li>Metallurgical Scrap Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The green geopolymer concrete market consists of two primary types: fly ash-based and metallurgical scrap-based. Fly ash-based geopolymer concrete utilizes industrial byproducts from coal combustion, enhancing sustainability and reducing waste. This type is widely adopted for its availability and effective binder properties. Metallurgical scrap-based geopolymer concrete involves repurposing byproducts from metal production, promoting resource efficiency and minimizing environmental impact. Both types contribute to eco-friendly construction practices, leveraging waste materials to create durable and sustainable concrete alternatives.</p></p>
