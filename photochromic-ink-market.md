<p><p>The photochromic ink market includes several specialized types. UV fluorescent inks change color under ultraviolet light, while thermochromatic inks respond to temperature variations. Optically variable inks exhibit color shifts based on viewing angles, and humidity-sensitive inks react to moisture levels. Infrared fluorescent inks are designed for infrared detection, enhancing security applications. Pressure-sensitive inks change properties when subjected to pressure. These diverse inks find applications in packaging, security, textiles, and promotional materials, providing innovative solutions for various industries.</p></p>
