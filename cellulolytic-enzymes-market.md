<p><ul><li>EG</li><li>CBH</li><li>BG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cellulolytic enzymes market encompasses three primary types: endoglucanases (EG), cellobiohydrolases (CBH), and beta-glucosidases (BG). EG enzymes disrupt the cellulose structure by cleaving internal bonds, enhancing substrate accessibility. CBH enzymes further break down cellooligosaccharides into cellobiose, facilitating more efficient degradation. BG enzymes convert cellobiose into glucose, completing the cellulose hydrolysis process. Together, these enzymes play a crucial role in various applications, including biofuel production, waste management, and textile processing, by optimizing cellulose breakdown and improving yield.</p></p>
