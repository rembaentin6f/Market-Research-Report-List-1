<p><strong>The Semiconductor IC Photomask Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quartz Mask</li><li>Soda Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor IC photomask market primarily consists of two types: quartz masks and soda-lime masks. Quartz masks are made from high-purity silica and are preferred for their durability and low thermal expansion, making them suitable for advanced lithography processes in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, soda-lime masks are more cost-effective and used for less critical applications. Both masks play essential roles in transferring circuit patterns onto semiconductor wafers, with their selection influenced by factors like production volume and technology requirements.</p></p>
