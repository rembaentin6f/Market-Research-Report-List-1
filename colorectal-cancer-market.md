<p><p>The colorectal cancer market comprises three primary treatment types: chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and immunotherapy. Chemotherapy uses cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells, often prescribed in various stages of the disease. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets associated with cancer growth, aiming to minimize damage to healthy cells. Immunotherapy leverages the body’s immune system to recognize and combat cancer cells, enhancing the immune response. Each treatment type plays a vital role in managing colorectal cancer, often used in combination for better outcomes.</p></p>
