<p><p>The non-metallic industrial gasket market comprises various types designed for specific applications. Spiral wound gaskets feature tightly wound metal strips with a non-metallic filler, providing excellent sealing capabilities. Ring joint gaskets are designed for high-pressure applications, offering a reliable seal through their unique shape. Corrugated gaskets, made from layered materials, provide flexibility and resilience in sealing irregular surfaces. Other types include soft cut gaskets and compressible materials, serving diverse industrial needs for reliable and efficient sealing solutions.</p></p>
