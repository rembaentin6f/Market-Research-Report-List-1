<p><ul><li>Sintered Rare Earth Magnet</li><li>Bonded Rare Earth Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rare Earth Magnet market for electric vehicles is primarily segmented into sintered and bonded magnets. Sintered rare earth magnets are made by compacting and sintering powdered materials, resulting in high performance and energy density, making them ideal for EV applications. In contrast, bonded rare earth magnets are created by mixing magnetic powders with a polymer or resin, offering flexibility in design and manufacturing. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and performance of electric vehicle motors and systems.</p></p>
