<p><p>The suture needles market includes various types designed for specific surgical applications. Round-bodied needles are used for delicate tissues, minimizing trauma. Blunt point needles are ideal for suturing organs where precision is crucial, reducing injury risk. Reverse cutting needles feature a cutting edge on the inside, suitable for tough tissues, while conventional cutting needles have a triangular cutting edge, effective for thicker skin. Spatula needles are employed in ophthalmic procedures, and tapercut needles combine features for versatility in suturing.</p></p>
