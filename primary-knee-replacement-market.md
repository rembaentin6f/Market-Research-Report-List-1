<p><p>The primary knee replacement market consists of various components, including the femoral, tibial, patellar, and other elements. The femoral component replaces the thigh bone's end, ensuring joint stability. The tibial component substitutes the upper part of the shin bone, facilitating motion. The patellar component addresses the kneecap, promoting smooth articulation. Other components may include fixation devices and instruments. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing knee functionality and patient mobility, driving demand in the orthopedic market.</p></p>
