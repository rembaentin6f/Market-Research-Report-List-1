<p><p>Privileged User Password Management (PUPM) involves securing and overseeing passwords and credentials associated with privileged accounts within an organization. These accounts typically have elevated access, making them critical for maintaining the integrity and security of sensitive data and systems. PUPM solutions help organizations mitigate risks associated with unauthorized access, insider threats, and compliance violations by offering features such as password vaulting, automated password rotation, and detailed audit trails.</p><p>The Privileged User Password Management Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing frequency of cyberattacks and the growing need for organizations to secure sensitive information. The adoption of stringent regulatory frameworks and compliance mandates further fuels this trend, as businesses seek to implement robust security measures. Additionally, the rise of remote work and cloud computing has diversified the attack surface, necessitating enhanced privileged access security.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period.
