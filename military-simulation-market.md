<p><p>The military simulation market is categorized into three main types: Live Training, Virtual Training, and Constructive Training. Live Training involves real personnel and equipment in actual environments, enhancing realism. Virtual Training uses computer-based simulations to create immersive scenarios without physical risks, allowing for flexibility and repeatability. Constructive Training employs simulations that use models and algorithms to replicate battlefield conditions, often involving multiple participants in a virtual setting, fostering strategic decision-making and teamwork without real-world constraints.</p></p>
