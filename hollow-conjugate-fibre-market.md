<p><p>The hollow conjugate fibre market is divided into two main types: siliconized fibre and non-silicified fibre. Siliconized fibre features a silicone coating that enhances smoothness and reduces friction, making it ideal for applications requiring a soft touch and durability, like bedding and apparel. In contrast, non-silicified fibre lacks this coating, offering benefits such as higher absorbency and greater dye retention, making it suitable for products like medical textiles and insulation materials. Both types cater to different customer needs and applications.</p></p>
