<p><p>The automotive cooling fan coupling market is divided into two primary types: plate sensor system type and coil sensor system type. The plate sensor system uses a flat plate mechanism to detect temperature changes, providing precise control over fan engagement. In contrast, the coil sensor system employs electromagnetic coils to regulate fan operation based on engine temperature, ensuring optimal cooling. Both systems are crucial for enhancing engine efficiency, reducing fuel consumption, and maintaining vehicle performance under varying conditions.</p></p>
