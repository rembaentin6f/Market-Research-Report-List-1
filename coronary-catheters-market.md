<p><p>The coronary catheters market is segmented into several types, including aspiration catheters, coronary balloon catheters, diagnostic catheters, and guide catheters. Aspiration catheters are used to remove blockages in coronary arteries. Coronary balloon catheters are employed to dilate narrowed vessels during angioplasty procedures. Diagnostic catheters facilitate the assessment of coronary artery conditions by delivering contrast agents for imaging. Guide catheters help in navigating other devices to the target site within the coronary arteries, enhancing procedural efficiency and precision.</p></p>
