<p><p>The eco-friendly RFID tags market comprises three main types: active, passive, and semi-passive. Active RFID tags have their own power source, enabling longer ranges and real-time tracking; however, their environmental impact is higher due to batteries. Passive RFID tags lack a power source, relying on readers for operation, making them more eco-friendly and suitable for various applications. Semi-passive tags combine features of both, using batteries for enhanced functionality while remaining more sustainable than active tags.</p></p>
