<p><p>The carton sealer market comprises two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic carton sealers. Fully automatic carton sealers streamline packaging processes by automatically forming, sealing, and labeling cartons without manual intervention, ideal for high-volume production environments. In contrast, semi-automatic carton sealers require some manual operation, such as folding flaps or placing the carton, making them suitable for smaller operations or lower volumes. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve packaging consistency.</p></p>
