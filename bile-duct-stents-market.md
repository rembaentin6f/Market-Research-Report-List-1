<p><strong>The Bile Duct Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Covered Type</li><li>Uncovered Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bile duct stents market is primarily divided into two types: covered and uncovered stents. Covered stents are designed with a polymer or mesh coating that prevents tissue ingrowth, enhancing patency and reducing the risk of blockage. In contrast, uncovered stents allow for natural tissue infiltration, which can promote stability but may lead to obstructions over time. Both types serve crucial roles in managing bile duct obstructions, with the choice depending on patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
