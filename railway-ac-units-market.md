<p><ul><li>Centralized</li><li>Local Centralized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the railway AC units market, there are primarily two types: centralized and local systems. Centralized systems use a single, large unit to cool or heat multiple cars or entire trains, optimizing energy efficiency and maintenance. Conversely, local systems feature individual units installed in each coach, allowing for tailored temperature control and comfort but potentially increasing energy consumption. Both types cater to different needs, balancing efficiency, operational complexity, and passenger comfort within the railway environment.</p></p>
