<p><p>The peppermint oil market is primarily divided into two types: natural and synthetic. Natural peppermint oil is derived from the steam distillation of peppermint leaves, offering a pure, aromatic essence valued in aromatherapy, cosmetics, and food industries. Synthetic peppermint oil, on the other hand, is chemically produced to mimic the scent and flavor of natural peppermint, often used in pharmaceuticals and cheaper consumer products. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and applications, impacting pricing and market dynamics.</p></p>
