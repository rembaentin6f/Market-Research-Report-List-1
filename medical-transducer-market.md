<p><strong>The Medical Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Convex Type</li><li>Phased Array Type</li><li>Endocavitary Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical transducer market comprises various types based on their design and application. Linear type transducers feature a straight array of crystals, ideal for vascular and superficial imaging. Convex type transducers have a curved array, offering broader imaging fields, suitable for abdominal evaluations. Phased array transducers use small, closely spaced elements, serving well in cardiac imaging due to their ability to manipulate the beam angle. Endocavitary transducers are designed for internal examinations, such as transvaginal or transrectal scans. Additionally, "others" encompass specialized and emerging transducer technologies.</p></p>
