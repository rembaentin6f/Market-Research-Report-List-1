<p><p>The Fluorescent Immunoassay market includes two primary types: ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) and rapid tests. ELISA is a laboratory technique that quantifies proteins or antigens with high sensitivity and specificity, typically requiring longer processing times. In contrast, rapid tests provide quick results, often within minutes, making them suitable for point-of-care diagnostics. Both methods utilize fluorescent labels to enhance detection, addressing various clinical needs from extensive research applications to immediate diagnostic purposes in diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
