Milk chocolate liquid extract combines cocoa solids with milk and sugar, providing a balanced taste that's rich yet mild. Dark chocolate liquid extract is rich in cocoa solids and has little to no milk, delivering a more intense and bittersweet flavor profile, appealing to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
