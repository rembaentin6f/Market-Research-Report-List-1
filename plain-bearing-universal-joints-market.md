<p><p>The Plain Bearing Universal Joints market includes single and double plain bearing types, which are crucial for transmitting torque in applications where shafts are not in a straight line. Single plain bearing universal joints incorporate one set of bearings, allowing for more compact designs, ideal for lower torque applications.
