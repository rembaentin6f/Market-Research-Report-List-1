<p><p>The diving gloves market is primarily divided into two types: wet and dry gloves. Wet gloves are designed for use in colder waters, providing insulation while allowing some water to enter, which warms up against the skin. They are typically made from neoprene. In contrast, dry gloves are intended for extremely cold or icy conditions, offering a waterproof barrier that keeps hands completely dry. Both types cater to diverse diving environments and preferences, enhancing grip and protection for divers.</p></p>
