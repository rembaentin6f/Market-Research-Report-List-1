<p><p>The Endometriosis Treatment Market includes several types of therapies. Hormonal contraceptives, such as birth control pills, help regulate the menstrual cycle and reduce pain by suppressing ovulation. Progesterone replacement therapy mimics the effects of pregnancy, minimizing endometrial tissue growth. GnRH analogues are medications that inhibit estrogen production, inducing a temporary menopause-like state to alleviate symptoms. Together, these treatment options aim to manage pain, reduce endometriosis-related complications, and improve the quality of life for affected individuals.</p></p>
