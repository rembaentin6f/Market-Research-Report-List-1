In contrast, non-invasive ventilators utilize masks or nasal interfaces to deliver respiratory support without invasive procedures, making them suitable for less severe cases. Both types are essential for ensuring optimal respiratory function in newborns during transport or in various healthcare settings, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
