<p><ul><li>Resistive Load Bank</li><li>Reactive Load Bank</li><li>Resistive/Reactive Load Bank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The load bank rental market consists of three main types: resistive load banks, reactive load banks, and resistive/reactive load banks. Resistive load banks simulate purely resistive electrical loads, ideal for testing generators and UPS systems. Reactive load banks incorporate inductive or capacitive components, useful for assessing power factor and system stability. Resistive/reactive load banks combine both load types, providing versatile testing capabilities for complex systems. Each type serves distinct testing needs in ensuring reliable and efficient power generation and distribution.</p></p>
