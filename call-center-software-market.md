<p><p>The call center software market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **On-premise call center software** is installed locally on company servers, providing complete control but requiring significant IT resources. </p><p>2. **Hosted call center software** is maintained by a third-party provider, offering the benefits of outsourcing without heavy infrastructure costs. </p><p>3. **Cloud-based call center software** operates via the internet, allowing for scalable solutions and remote access. </p><p>4. **Browser-based call center software** runs directly in web browsers, simplifying usage and minimizing installation requirements.</p></p>
