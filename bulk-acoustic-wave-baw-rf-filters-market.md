<p><p>Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) RF filters are specialized components used in telecommunications to enhance signal integrity by filtering unwanted frequencies. The market is primarily divided into two types: Film Bulk Acoustic Resonators (FBAR) and Surface Mounted Resonators (SMR). FBAR filters utilize thin piezoelectric films to create high-frequency responses, ideal for compact applications, while SMR filters leverage surface acoustic waves for broader performance. Both types are crucial for 5G and advanced wireless communication systems, addressing the growing demand for efficient filtering solutions.</p></p>
