<p><strong>The Nuclear Medicine System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) Systems</li><li>Hybrid Positron Emission Tomography (PET) Systems</li><li>Planar Scintigraphy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear medicine systems encompass several imaging technologies used for diagnosing and monitoring diseases. Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) systems utilize gamma rays to create 3D images of radiotracer distribution within the body, offering insights into tissue function. Hybrid Positron Emission Tomography (PET) systems combine PET with CT or MRI to provide detailed anatomical and functional information. Planar scintigraphy involves capturing 2D images of radioisotope distribution, primarily for assessing organ function and detecting abnormalities. Each type plays a crucial role in clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
