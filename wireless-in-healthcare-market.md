<p><p>The wireless healthcare market comprises various technologies tailored for medical applications. Bluetooth is used for short-range device connectivity, like wearables. Zigbee supports low-power, low-data-rate communication for sensors. RFID enables tracking and identification of medical assets. UWB offers high-accuracy location tracking in clinical environments. WLAN provides reliable local network access for health information sharing, while WMAN facilitates broader connectivity for hospitals or healthcare facilities. WWAN enables mobile healthcare solutions by providing wide-area coverage for remote patient monitoring and telemedicine applications.</p></p>
