<p><p>The upper extremity bone fixation screws market comprises three primary types: stainless steel, titanium, and bioabsorbable screws. Stainless steel screws are favored for their strength and durability, making them ideal for various orthopedic applications. Titanium screws offer a lightweight alternative with excellent biocompatibility, reducing the risk of allergic reactions. Bioabsorbable screws, made from polymers that dissolve over time, provide temporary fixation, eliminating the need for subsequent removal surgery. Each type serves specific clinical needs and patient considerations in orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
