<p><strong>The Infrared Camera Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shortwave</li><li>Medium Wave</li><li>Long Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared camera modules are classified into three types based on their wavelength sensitivity: shortwave, medium wave, and long wave. Shortwave infrared cameras (SWIR) operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometer range, ideal for imaging through fog or smoke. Medium wave infrared cameras (MWIR) function between 3 to 5 micrometers, often utilized in surveillance and industrial applications. Long wave infrared cameras (LWIR), operating from 8 to 14 micrometers, are commonly used for thermal imaging, enabling temperature measurement and heat detection in various environments.</p></p>
